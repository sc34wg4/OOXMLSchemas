Specification

At present, ISO/IEC 29500 consists of three sets of documents.

  • The original multi-part standard: ISO/IEC 29500-1:2008, ISO/IEC 29500-2:2008, ISO/IEC 29500-3:2008, and ISO/IEC 29500-4:2008. They are freely available at http://standards.iso.org/ittf/PubliclyAvailableStandards/index.html.
  • The first set of technical corrigenda to the original: ISO/IEC 29500-1:2008/Cor 1:2010, ISO/IEC 29500-2:2008/Cor 1:2010, ISO/IEC 29500-3:2008/Cor 1:2010, and ISO/IEC 29500-4:2008/Cor 1:2010. They are freely available at ISO Store.
  • The first set of amendments to the original: ISO/IEC 29500-1:2008/Amd 1:2010 and ISO/IEC 29500-4:2008/Amd 1:2010. They are freely available at http://standards.iso.org/ittf/PubliclyAvailableStandards/index.html.

There is a plan to publish consolidated specifications, but WG4 found small conflicts between technical corrigenda and amendments. To resolve these conflicts, SC34 will first create two technical corrigenda for fixing amendments.

On-line schemas

Schemas are available at a SVN repository.

On-going projects

Three projects are in progress. Two of them are amendments of the original standards for addressing issues around ISO 8601 date and time. The third project is to create a new multi-part standard for extending OOXML.

Documents under ballots

The ballots for a draft technical corrigenda to ISO/IEC 29500-1:2008/Amd.1:2010 and another to ISO/IEC 29500-4:2008/Amd.1:2010 will start soon. These DCORs will resolve the conflicts mentioned above.
